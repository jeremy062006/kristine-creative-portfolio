About Me

I am a recent graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Agroforestry, and I am excited to begin my journey as an educator in the field of agriculture and environmental studies. Throughout my academic years, I have gained strong knowledge in sustainable land use, forest management, and agricultural systems that I am eager to share with future learners.

During my practice teaching, I discovered how fulfilling it is to guide students in understanding the importance of agroforestry and environmental stewardship. I enjoyed seeing their curiosity and confidence grow as they learned how trees, crops, and people can coexist in harmony. Those experiences deepened my passion for teaching and inspired me to continue developing as an Agroforestry educator.

I am committed to creating a learning environment that is welcoming, hands-on, and engaging, where students can connect theory with real-world applications.
